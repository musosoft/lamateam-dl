		Riverwalk
  Riverwalk Village - Defuse Scenario

Counter-Terrorists: 
Defend the town bridge (A) and the shopping 
market (B) from the terrorists who plan to 
disrupt economic and recreation patterns for 
tourists

Terrorists: 
Navigate your way through the riverwalk 
area to destroy the main town bridge (A) or
the decadent shopping plaza (B) to economically
ruin the shopping district for this town.
 
Other Notes: There are two objective sites
within this level.
